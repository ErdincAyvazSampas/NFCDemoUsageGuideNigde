NFC Demo Usage Guide

Step 1: Swipe left to switch to the second menu when the app opens.

Step 2: Select the "Su Yükleme" menu.

Step 3: On this screen, shake your phone and a red info button will appear in the bottom right corner. Tap this button.

Step 4: Enter user information and activate guest mode.

Step 5: Select "BAYLAN" from the list.

Step 6: Tap the "Scan Demo Card" button to read the card information.

Step 7: Tap the "Bakiye Yükle" button.

Step 8: Select "TL Yükle", enter 1 into the "Diğer Tutar giriniz" field, then tap the "Yükle" button.

Step 9: Tap the "Ödeme Yap" button.

Step 10: Tap the "Make Demo Payment" button.

Step 11: Tap the "Scan Demo Card" button to write information to the card.
